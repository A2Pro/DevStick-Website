Return Policy

Product returns will be accepted under the following conditions:

  1. The product must be demonstrated to be materially defective or non-functioning as intended upon receipt;
  2. Documentation of the defect must be provided, including but not limited to photographic or video evidence;
  3. Claims must be submitted within 30 days of receipt of the product;
  4. The product must be returned in its original packaging with all included components.

To initiate a return, please contact aayushpalai@gmail.com. You will be provided with return shipping instructions and a return authorization number.

Refund Policy

Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. When approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days of receiving and verifying the returned product. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
